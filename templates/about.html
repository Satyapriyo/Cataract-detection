About Us



We are a team of Students who are passionate about using artificial intelligence. Our website allows you to upload an image of your eye and get a diagnosis of cataract, a common eye condition that causes blurred vision and can lead to blindness if left untreated.



Our website uses a deep learning model that we trained on thousands of images of eyes with and without cataract. The model can detect cataract with high accuracy and speed. We hope that our website can help people who have limited access to eye care or who want to check their eye health regularly.
